📋 How to Launch Daily
💻 PC or Mac
  1. Open tntechno.github.io/ai-pulse in your browser
  2. Press Ctrl+D (PC) or Cmd+D (Mac) to bookmark it
  3. Each morning, click the bookmark and tap Refresh Brief
🤖 Pixel / Android
  1. Open tntechno.github.io/ai-pulse in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu → "Add to Home screen"
  3. An app icon appears — tap it each morning, then tap Refresh Brief
🍎 iPhone
  1. Open tntechno.github.io/ai-pulse in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap the icon each morning, then tap Refresh Brief
💡 Tip: Tap "Refresh Brief" every morning for a fresh AI-generated analysis of that day's job market developments.
